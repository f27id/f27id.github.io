Today's lecture will explain what this course is about and what to expect. For example, the syllabus, teaching schedule and how you'll be assessed. We'll also provide some simple examples on interaction design to help you see why the topic is so important (that is good and bad). We'll finish up the lecture with a set of recommended reading and closing discussion of the material.
At the end of this course, you should have mastered certain concepts. You should be able to critically assess interfaces. Address and propose solutions for problem interfaces. Evaluate the effectiveness of an interface.
