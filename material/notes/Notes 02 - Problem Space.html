# Notes - Problem Space Supplementary notes to complement the lecture material for Introduction to Interaction Design (F27ID) - **Problem Space**. # Understanding the problem space Problem space - Understand and conceptualize what is currently the user experience/product and how this is going to be improved or changed. Different people have different perspectives, defining a problem space is usually best done by a group of different individuals. You need to consider: * Are there problems with an existing product or user experience? * Why do you think there are problems? * How do you think your proposed design ideas might overcome these? * If you have not identified any problems and instead are designing for a new user experience how do you think your proposed design ideas support, change, or extend current ways of doing things?
