# Lab - Usability/Design Lab exercises to help you master the subject material and to complement the lecture material for Introduction to Interaction Design (F27ID). #Overview This lab provides activities to assist you in preparing and finalizing a prototype that takes into account usability and user-experience. Revisit the problem space to incorpororate additional information you've learned on usability and user-experience. These exercises help you develop critical thinking for your specific design/problem space/task you developed earlier in the labs. # Exercise 1 Describe three scenarios for your problem space to help you clarifiy how the user will interacts with your design. # Exercise 2 Describe one out of at least three scenarios in detail. Then perform task analysis for each of the scenarios (connect this to the problem space). # Exercise 3 Develop a detailed outline/design based on your design scenarios (again take into acount any details/limits from your problem space). # Exercise 4 Sketch the wireframe for one of your scenario for your design (again using your problem space as guidance).
